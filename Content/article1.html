When is Blue Secrets Season 2 coming?

In this drama-filled show, Blue Secrets where Tony is a regular person, but another title is named “Keeper of Secrets”. He runs a website called Blue Secrets where people can write anonymously to him so they can let out their emotions. Sometimes Tony would drop his two cents if it is a problem. Some say the website name came from “it is like a dropping your worries into the ocean.” To make sure, Tony promised his visitors that if any of their secrets get leaked, he will shut down the site, apologize publicly, and hand himself to the police.

Each episode follows a visitor to the site, and what their secret is, Tony comments his opinion or interferes some other way, and the visitor changes for the better. However, the pattern was changed when the visitor of the last episode got her secret leaked by a third party and Tony is behind bars. Now the past visitors are banding together to find out who is the third party and clear Tony’s name.

When is Season 2?

Since season 1 end on a cliffhanger, some speculate that season 2 would come shortly after. However, the finale of season 1 happened in late 2019. Rumors popped up in late 2020 and late 2021 but they didn’t hold up. People are optimistic about Blue Secrets returning in the future and we will keep you updated!
