You Won't Believe What Issac Alsop Did

Issac Alsop is a 22-year-old actor that is adored by many. Once a small-time actor who only land minor roles but got his big break with a show called “Blue Secrets” as Tony the lead character. Now the actor is landing big roles one after another and even has a podcast to advise aspiring actors. People around him say he is a nice guy who always greets people around him and is a joy to be around.

However, Issac Alsop did something that is quite a shocker to everyone. On Friday, old messages from 2015 of Alsop saying bad stuff. Like “I hope Zack loses his spot in the play so I can take his spot.” and “I hope Zack gets sick so I can get the lead spot in the play.” Alsop was an understudy who wanted the lead role in his school play so much that he wished sickness on his classmate.

Now Alsop apologizes for the messages saying, “I was a stupid kid who was too ambitious that I wish my classmate didn’t have the lead role. I am truly sorry.” The public reception is mixed, some say Alsop didn’t need to apologize because it happened a long time ago, while others are not convinced that Alsop was truly sorry due to him being an actor.
